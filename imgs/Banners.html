    Web Banners by Canva

    A good online banner can be just as head-turning as an eye-catching billboard. Imagine the amount of articles, websites and blog pages your readers scroll through everyday. Set yourself apart from the rest of the generic online ads with some beautiful and expertly-designed banners that will catch your audience's attention and attracts them enough to click and read more.

    Coming up with such a banner need not be expensive or time-consuming. Canva's wide collection of online banner templates, designed by professional graphic artists, are all you need to keep your website, social media account or blog updated. You won't even need to worry about the varying dimensions needed for different platforms. The banner layouts already formatted with the correct size. Take your pick of layouts for Facebook ads, Twitter, Tumblr and Etsy store headers, Google+ covers, YouTube channel art and email headers. Online ad formats include medium and large rectangles, leaderboards and wide skyscrapers.

    With Canva's remarkable library of over a million stock photographs, illustrations and vectors – both free and premium – you have your pick of the best graphics to use. Type in your tagline or message using Canva's collection of beautiful fonts. You also have the ability to collaborate with your partners by sending them a link to access your editable design. Download your design as a JPEG or PNG file, or share it on your social media account straight from Canva. Edit your design any time by finding it under "My Designs" in your Canva homepage.
